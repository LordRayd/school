Dark Net

 

 

Problematic

 

         Did the dark net is a bad thing?

 

Origins

 

In the 1970s, during the ARPANET (Advanced Research Projects Agency Network), who is the base of data transfer with packet, begins, another side of the net appears, its the darknet also call Dark Net. The dark net can see all the data of the Arpanet but the reverse is not true. The hidden networks cant appear on the Arpanet and wouldnt answer to the ping and some other inquiries. It provides anonymity for the users.

In 2002, Peter Biddle, Paul England, Marcus Peinado and Bryan Willman are four Microsoft employees who wrote a book name The Darknet and the Future of Content Distribution. This book will contribute to the development of the acknowledgement of the Darknet.

In 2010, the word darknet became usual because he is often use by the media and the public well know the darknet with some browser like TOR who allow to find the site host in the Darknet.

 

Users

 

On internet, we are follow and its a fact that everyone knows. Some people who dont want to be monitoring by the government and the GAFAs use the Darknet to become invisible and obtain a freedom lost by the large public. The users are different, most of them are criminal, they look for some illegal content because we can find pedophile material, drugs and other stuff on darknet. There are also the whistleblowers who want to speak freely and alert everyone with their worry and theory.

 

How to access

 

You can access to the darknet from your computer. All you must do is to download a browser who search in the darknet like tor who is the most popular on the market, but he is not the only one, there is many of them like Freenet, OneSwarm, RetroShare or Tribler. We only know the most popular of them however there are secret browser and technique unknow by the public.

 

Conclusion

 

         The darknet is not a good or a bad thing, the nature of it depends on the people who use it and on the content they post and they want to see.
